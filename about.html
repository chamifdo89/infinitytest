Our Journey

Infinity Customs is Lower Hutt's premier choice for automotive detailing and care. We established this business with a passion for perfection and a commitment to restoring every vehicle to its showroom glory.

Based in Taita, we offer both a fully equipped workshop and a convenient mobile service that comes to you. From deep interior cleans to high-end ceramic coatings, we treat your vehicle with the utmost respect and precision.
